An amount LET is being divided between all posters every period according to fixed emission,

First period of the month starts from 1 day of the month midnight utc and ending on day 15 of the month 23:59:59 pm by UTC(Greenwich)
Second period starts after that and lasts up to the last day of the month 23:59:59 pm by UTC(Greenwich)
The less post in a period - the more tokens each posts costs.
Starting from the website modifier, like 4chan is being paid 10x more than any other imageboard as of now, then comes humanness modifier for every poster to compute how exactly rewards will be divided.

What is humanness?

Humanness level is subjective and attempts to measure how much of a bot/AI particular poster is. Current humanness levels are the most basic and mild in penalties and bonuses.
All new posters start with base humanness of 2.
It can be decreased due to consistent low-effort spam
If humanness is reduced to 1, poster earns 15% less rewards than level 2 humanness
If humanness is reduced to 0, poster earns 30% less rewards than level 2 humanness
Humanness level 3 is granted for good sense of humor and interesting and/or valuable posting and gives +15% rewards for every post.
Humanness is different for different languages, and currently exists only for English, in all other languages posts are so far get paid equally
Humanness is currently mild, since fundamental value of a bump on /biz/ is still high as there are not enough posters, but with time and established userbase the metric will become more and more complex and harsh
